FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise accounts. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway with PCI DSS compliance.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time with just a few clicks. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges or credits based on your current billing cycle. There are no penalties for switching plans.
What is your uptime guarantee?
We guarantee 99.99% uptime for all our hosting plans. Our infrastructure is built on enterprise-grade cloud providers with redundant systems, automatic failover, and 24/7 monitoring to ensure maximum availability. If we fail to meet this guarantee, you'll receive service credits.
Do you offer migration assistance?
Yes, our team provides free migration assistance for all new customers. We'll handle the entire migration process including transferring your files, databases, and configurations to ensure a smooth transition with zero downtime. Our experts will work with you every step of the way.
What kind of support do you provide?
We offer 24/7 support via email and live chat for all plans. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times (average 7 minutes for emergencies), phone support, and dedicated account managers for Enterprise customers. All our support staff are experienced system administrators.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new hosting plans. If you're not completely satisfied with our service for any reason, you can request a full refund within the first 30 days of your subscription. No questions asked, no hassle.
How secure is my data with Corefinity?
Your data security is our top priority. We provide PCI compliant infrastructure, DDoS protection, free SSL certificates, advanced firewalls, regular security audits, automated backups, and 24/7 security monitoring. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.
Can I host multiple websites on one plan?
Yes, all our plans support multiple websites and domains. The number of sites you can host depends on your resource allocation (CPU, RAM, storage). Professional and Enterprise plans are optimized for hosting multiple high-traffic websites with dedicated resources and load balancing.
