Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hosting plans

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time. The changes will be reflected immediately, and billing will be adjusted on a pro-rata basis. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any plan changes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not completely satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.
Do you offer migration services?
Absolutely! We provide free website migration for all new customers. Our expert team will handle the entire migration process, ensuring zero downtime and complete data integrity.
What kind of support do you provide?
All plans include 24/7 support via live chat, email, and phone. Professional and Enterprise plans receive priority and dedicated support respectively, with faster response times and dedicated account managers.
Are backups included in all plans?
Yes, all plans include automated backups. Starter and Professional plans receive daily backups retained for 30 days, while Enterprise plans get hourly backups with 90-day retention. All backups are stored in multiple secure locations.
Can I host multiple Magento stores?
Yes, Professional and Enterprise plans support multiple Magento installations. The number of stores you can host depends on your resource allocation and traffic requirements. Contact our team for custom multi-store solutions.
What makes Corefinity different from other hosts?
Corefinity specializes exclusively in Magento hosting. Our infrastructure is optimized specifically for Magento, our support team is Magento-certified, and we provide tools and features tailored to e-commerce success. This specialization means better performance, security, and support for your store.
